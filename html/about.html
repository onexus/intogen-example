The Biomedical Genomics Group

BG Group is a research group integrated in the Research Unit on Biomedical Informatics (GRIB) of the University Pompeu Fabra (UPF). The lab is located in the newly formed Biomedical Research Park in Barcelona (PRBB).

Our main research interest is the computational study of human genetic diseases, including hereditary diseases and cancer, at genomic level (see details in Projects).
