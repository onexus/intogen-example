Explore results in IntOGen browser

The results obtained from the analysis are displayed in IntOGen browser which contain various options to visualize the results interactively in the form of Tables, Heatmaps and Plots. The results can be easily downloaded in text files for further analysis elsewhere.

Importantly one interesting option provided by IntOGen browser is to compare your results with the results of previous sequencing projects. You can easily explore if the genes mutated in your cohort of tumors are mutated in other tumors for example.
